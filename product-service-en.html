Festival Attendance and Attractions by Neighborhood

This chart combines festival attendance and the number of attractions across different neighborhoods in Catalonia. This dual-layered analysis is crucial for our AI pricing model, which predicts BNB prices based on expected tourist inflows, which are influenced by local attractions and events. The disparity in festival attendance, despite the number of attractions, provides insights into external factors that may affect BNB pricing, such as accessibility, seasonal popularity, and local infrastructure. This nuanced understanding enhances the predictive accuracy of our AI model.

Relationship Between Star Ratings and Average Prices

This scatter plot explores the correlation between the average star ratings of accommodations and their respective average pricing in Catalonia.

Data Labeling

Our company has close cooperation with many top laboratories in academia or top companies in the industry and maintains in-depth exchanges with the most senior scholars, professors, and research institutes in neuroscience and artificial intelligence. Detailed and complete 3D reconstruction of different brain regions (such as the cerebellum, visual cortex, and prefrontal cortex) in different species (such as mice, rats, and humans) were performed at the nanoscale to obtain a map of neural connections (also known as [connectome]) of these brain tissue samples. The main work is manual annotation and 3D reconstruction to calibrate UHD image data sets. The delicate link maps of neural networks in geometry and topology, which can pave the way for the next generation of artificial intelligence, have never been obtained.
